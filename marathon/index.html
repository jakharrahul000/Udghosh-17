Race Information


  • - Date : 10 September, 2017 | Sunday
  • - Venue : Green Park Stadium, Kanpur
  • - Time : 06 : 00 AM
  • - Length : 5 km track
  • - No Registration Fee.
  • - Certificates and goodies for all the participants.

Facilities


  • 1. Medical Facilities - We will have enough number of ambulances with us for any medical problem. Ambulance will have all the basic medical facilities.
  • 2. Backing vehicles - If any of the participants gets exhausted, we will provide vehicles for them to reach to final destination.
  • 3. Refreshments - The refreshments will be provided at the final destination for each and every participant.
  • 4. Basic - Drinking water, toilets and all other facilities will be provided by us.

“The miracle isn’t that I finished. It’s that I had the courage to start.”
Even after 70 years of independence, Kanpur belongs to the list of tier 2 cities. Who better than us to help change this impasse. Being an institute of national importance, Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur considers it as its responsibility to spread the message
“Sore today Strong tomorrow”
In collaboration with the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, UDGHOSH 2017 presents to you the Kanpur Marathon. So, let’s run for the beginning of a change. Gear up Kanpurites, because its the time to run, to choose fitness as a way of life, and to champion the spirit of a clean and green Kanpur.
